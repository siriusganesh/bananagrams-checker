Bananagrams Word Checker

Validates against a Scrabble-style word list and finds every legal word you can build from your letters.

Full anagrams (uses all letters)

Sub-anagrams (any subset of letters)

Word list: ~279k words (Collins/SOWPODS-derived superset that includes all TWL Scrabble words). See the README on the repo for how to swap in a different list.
